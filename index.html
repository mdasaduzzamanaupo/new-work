Minority Athletes Coalition

As the mother of two black college athletesit has been disheartening to here theseyoung men come forwardwith their stories ofinequalityand mistreatment based on there skin color. The one consistent theme i have heard and read amongst their outcries was lack of support, space to be heard, and action taken when they have voiced it . i see a lot of people saying why now , what changed why are you just saying something ? The reality is a lot these caaches hold keys to these athletes future , their education , and fore some the path to the next level of football (NFL). That’s a lot of power when in the wrong hands . As a result these guy stay silent , they conform and do what is needed to reach their goal. we see it everyday when a player voices their stance they are told to shut up and dribble, or i saw a fan tell one kid as a free educatuion is not enough? The fan then proceeded to tell the kid to be gratful and shut up and play !(Yikes) I digress. The reality is their is nothing free about the education given. Colleges and Universities make millions on the books of these athletes, and they work harder than the average student. sometimes my kid has 12 hour days because aside from team meetings, weightlifting,practice and playing in games they have the same expactations to perform academicaly I want say to the guys both past and present that we hear you ,but i also hear the cries of these all over the world . However, we are in a space and time where listening without putting forward action is no longer acceptable . so i have decided to start a not for profit organization called “The Minority Athlete Coalition ”.One og the biggest goals will be to educate collegiate programs on racial sensitivity, and to help them develop an inclusive culture where all genders , and races are treated well, and experience the same equality as their counterparts. This will also serve inequality in their prospective program. This is my thought on paper but, I will be partnering with collegiate programs, athletes both amateur and professional so that we can eradicate this problem together !
